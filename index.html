How to Play Akari (Light Up)

  • Click on a white cell to place or remove a light bulb.
  • Each light bulb lights up its entire row and column until it hits a black cell or the edge of the grid.
  • No two light bulbs may shine on each other (i.e., no two bulbs can be in the same lit row or column).
  • All white cells must be lit.
  • Some black cells contain a number (0–4): this indicates how many light bulbs must be placed in the four adjacent cells (up, down, left, right).
  • Black cells without numbers do not have any restrictions, but they still block light.

Welcome to Akari!
This puzzle game is developed in Haskell and compiled to WebAssembly to run seamlessly in your browser.
